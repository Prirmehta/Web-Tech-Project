The Library

Library and Information Centre [LIC] provides the ideal ambience for production & dissemination of knowledge, information, insights & intellect across schools of the University. The Library holds a huge collection of printed as well electronic resources which include books, journals, databases, CDs/DVDs, e-journals, reports, case studies, conference proceedings, training manuals, etc. The LIC is made user-friendly by using Information Technology services such as OPAC for checking online availability and online book reservation. It is ensured that the resources are easily accessible to the users, remote access to e-resources and online databases are also made available.
